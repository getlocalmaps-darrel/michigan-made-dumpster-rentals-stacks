Frequently Asked Questions — dumpster rental lincoln park

Q: What sizes of dumpsters does Michigan Made Dumpster Rentals offer?

A: We offer three types: rubber wheel trailers (5, 8, 10, 15, 20, and 25 yard), roll off dumpsters (10, 15, 20, 30, and 40 yard), and front load dumpsters (2, 4, 6, and 8 yard). Call (734) 346-0074 and we'll help you pick the right size for your project.

Q: What are rubber wheel trailer dumpsters?

A: Rubber wheel trailer dumpsters sit on pneumatic tires instead of being dropped directly on your driveway like traditional roll-offs. They won't crack, scratch, or damage your concrete or asphalt, and they're low to the ground for easy loading without a ramp. They're our most popular option for residential projects.

Q: Do you offer same-day dumpster delivery?

A: Yes — we offer same-day delivery throughout Wayne County and surrounding areas. Call (734) 346-0074 or text (734) 775-5778 to check availability.

Q: Is delivery and pickup free?

A: Yes. Free delivery and free pickup are included with every rental from Michigan Made Dumpster Rentals. No hidden fees.

Q: What areas do you serve?

A: We serve all of Wayne County including Brownstown Township, Flat Rock, Rockwood, Riverview, Allen Park, Trenton, Gibraltar, Dearborn, Lincoln Park, Taylor, Southgate, Wyandotte, Woodhaven, Westland, Livonia, Canton, Romulus, and surrounding communities. We also serve Newport, Monroe County, and Ypsilanti.

Q: What can I put in a dumpster?

A: Most household and construction debris: furniture, appliances, drywall, roofing shingles, wood, yard waste, and general junk. Hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, batteries, and tires are not accepted. Call us if you're unsure about specific items.
